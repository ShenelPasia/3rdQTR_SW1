!! Nana !!

“The dreams we are chasing and the reality that is chasing us are always parallel; they never meet.”

Japanese manga writer and artist Ai Yazawa is the creator of the "Nana" manga. Later, it was transformed into an anime series. The narrative centers on two young women, both named Nana, who travel to Tokyo in search of love and a chance to live out their ambitions. Nana Osaki is a punk rock singer with a harsh attitude and a difficult childhood, whereas Nana Komatsu, commonly known as Hachi, is a gentle and naive girl who falls in love easily. Despite having quite different personalities, the two eventually share a room and develop a great bond. Amid addition to exploring friendship, love, and the quest of bliss, the series also examines the ups and downs of life in Tokyo's vibrant music scene.
